3D_250cP

3D Printer for under 250cP materials

The idea is to design and fabricate a 3D printer with high precision allied to the ability to dispense paste like materials. This idea came from the need that exists on the Departement where i study and worked before, nowadays more than ever there's a search to find biocompatible materials and having them in a pasty consistency is ideal.

The design

Not the most regularly seem but one i think it will work, i inspired myself in using leadscrew rods in all axis in order to have a high precision with relatively moderade speed.

For now it won't be enclosed but if need be i will design some sort on protection to be possible to use a UV light source for a specific application.

The dispensor

I've design and used mechanical seringe dispensers but for this case i wanted to try some sort of pneumatic pump to push me material out without the need to use a mechanical device on the tip of the printer making it lighter and easier to move around. Not sure if that this may be the appropriate type of dispensor but it may work.

The electronics

Ideally the system will be independent from a computer, maybe have a LCD with a bottom to control the printer and some form of comunication either beeing USB, SD card, wifi or even bluetooth.
