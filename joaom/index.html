About me

João Milheiro

I'm a 37 years old computer engineer, and currently working on Fab Lab Aldeias do Xisto in Portugal. My areas of professional interest are 3d printing and modelling, CAD/CAM design, lasercutting, CNC routing, web design, software development, among others. During the free times, i like to bike, jog, play snooker, be with my family and do some crafts at home, recently related to digital fabrication. Hope this year's fabacademy give me the skills needed, especially in electronics, so that i can create almost anything, too.
